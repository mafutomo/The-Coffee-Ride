Costa Rica Sonoraclose

This Central American coffee is extremely special. Besides the fact that this coffee is a show stopping crowd pleaser, the Hacienda Sonora farm also takes full consideration in growing only the highest quality coffees with the most sustainable growing practices. Located on 55 acres, with 55 of those being dedicated to forest reserve, this farm is also home to a variety of species of birds and animals that seek refuge on its land.
